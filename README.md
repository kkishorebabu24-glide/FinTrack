# 📊 **FinTrack - Personal Finance Manager**

## 🎯 **What FinTrack Does**

FinTrack is a **complete personal finance management app** that helps you track money, manage tasks, and plan for the future - all in one place!

---

## ✨ **Core Features**

### **1. 💰 Dashboard - Financial Overview**
- **Monthly Income vs Expenses** at a glance
- **Net Worth** calculation (total income - total expenses)
- **Month Savings** tracking
- **Interactive Chart** showing 6-month income/expense trends
- Quick visual summary of your financial health

---

### **2. 📝 Daily Expenses Tracker**
- **Add Expenses** with:
  - Date
  - Category (Food, Transport, Bills, Entertainment, Shopping, Health, Other)
  - Amount
  - Optional note
- **View Recent Expenses** (last 15 entries)
- **Delete Expenses** you don't need
- **Add Income** with source details

---

### **3. ✅ Chores Management**
- **Create todo list** for daily tasks
- **Mark tasks as complete** (strikethrough effect)
- **Delete tasks** when done
- Perfect for tracking household chores or personal to-dos

---

### **4. 🇮🇳 Savings Checklist (Indian Context)**
Pre-built checklist of important financial goals:
- ✅ Emergency Fund (6 months expenses)
- ✅ Health Insurance
- ✅ Term Life Insurance
- ✅ PPF Account
- ✅ NPS (National Pension System)
- ✅ Sukanya Samriddhi Yojana
- ✅ Atal Pension Yojana
- ✅ PM Jeevan Jyoti Bima Yojana
- ✅ PM Suraksha Bima Yojana
- ✅ Fixed Deposits

**Tick items as you complete them** - visual progress tracking!

---

### **5. 🎯 Future Savings Goals**
- **Set financial goals** with:
  - Goal name
  - Target amount
  - Current savings
  - Deadline date
- **Visual progress bars** showing how close you are
- **Update current savings** anytime
- **Delete completed goals**
